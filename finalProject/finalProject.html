Twin Snakes

Controls:
Player 1 (red): WASD
Player 2 (blue): Arrow Keys

A local versus take on the classic Snake game. Duke it out between two players, controlling each snake with WASD and arrow key schemes respectively. Winning conditions go to the last who survives or the first to 10 plums.

Paddles

Controls:
Player 1 (red): W and D keys
Player 2 (blue): Up and down arrow keys

A two-player game inspired by Pong, hit the ball back and forth as it speeds up until the ball passes a player's goal. The W and D keys as well as the up and down arrow keys are used to control player 1 and player 2 respectively. First to five points wins.

Tag

Controls:
Player 1 (red): WASD
Player 2 (blue): Arrow Keys

An asymetric versus game where one player chases the other, losing health so long as they are "it". Player 1 start's as the "it" player, having to tag player 2 to make them "it". Both players a have full range movement with the WASD and the Arrow Keys respectively. When a player's health hits 0, the game ends.
